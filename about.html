About Me

I am Daria Orlova, a Digital Media artist with a focus on web development and interactive graphics. In a world where technology integrates itself further into our lives with every passing day, there is a growing surrealistic quality to exploring nature, and so my biggest inspiration is the fluidity and liveliness of the natural world.

I have always been passionate about the arts, and explored many different crafts and mediums that inform my digital work and processes today. I am the current Secretary of the CADRE Student Organization and participated at the club’s Maker Faire Bay Area 2024 Exhibit. I have also been involved in creating larger physical installations, such as the 2018 and 2019 LAVTA Wheels Bus Shelters throughout my hometown and the San Pedro Square Market Street Mural in 2024.
