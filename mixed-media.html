Mixed Media
Ferdinand's Room

A 3D modeled room inspired by a plush frog I had sewn for myself. A serene, warm glow from the lamp envelopes the room and establishes an inviting atmosphere as Ferdinand the Frog is perched upon a stool.

Created and rendered in Blender 4.2



The Visitor

A gigantic and monstrous earwig looms and dominates over the space of a small bedroom. In the fall of 2020, I was plagued by an infestation of the insects in my house for 3 months. I felt suffocated and unable to escape the bugs as they invaded every moment of my day. This digital painting offers the viewer a glimpse into my own harrowing experience.

Created using Adobe Photoshop



Little Guys

An edited photograph of mine where tiny plant spirits run amok. Mini terrariums are a passion of mine, and in my childhood, I often imagined small worlds of fantastical creatures coexisting with the bugs I could find.

Created using Adobe Photoshop and After Effects



Trilogy of Self

A cast aluminum medallion displaying a radial patterning that is broken up by 2 California Poppy flowers sculpted in relief. This medallion contains symbols pertaining to my heritage as a first-generation American who was raised within the culture of my parents at home, but also as a Californian in my daily life at school. These identities harmonize together in an homage to all which came before me, what I have accomplished so far, and the decisions I will make in the future.

Cast Aluminum with black patina and polished wax finish
