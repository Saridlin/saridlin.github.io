Crazy Painting!

Draw on the canvas using your mouse however you would like below!

To select a brush:
Press 1 for "Light Sticks".
Press 2 for "Cool Triangles".
Press 3 for "Inverted Spots".
Change your brush size using < to decrease, or > to increase.
Please reselect your brush or eraser choice after changing the size.

To select an eraser:
Press 9 for a solid eraser.
Press 0 for a more translucent eraser.

Change the canvas background by pressing Z or X.
Clear the canvas by pressing c.
Save your creation by pressing p.
